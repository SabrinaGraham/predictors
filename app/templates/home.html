Overview

This platform was designed and implemented by a team of university computing majors to help combat the crime and violence issue within our communities in Jamaica. They have created this application with the user in mind. This is a secure and reliable application based on Artificial Intelligence (AI) models. It provides crime reporting, news, analytics and prediction resources to its registered users. The application utilizes data collected from STATIN, JCF and other reputable statistics collection agencies.

How can you help?

>This application allows its users to contribute to the crime prevention and mitigation initiative within our country. Users may utilize the crime reporting form to submit reports to the relevant security authorities for investigation and tracking. This allows for a more faster, efficient and convenient crime detection process. Therefore, the submission of crime reports is paramount to the cause!

How to stay safe?

The user's safety is of utmost importance and as such is our first priority in the implementation of this application. We encourage all our users and stakeholders to utilize the data and resources presented here for their safety and consideration. The app provides relevant crime information of specific divisions within Jamaica that the user can use as reference point and to remind them to stay vigilant in in crime prone areas. This is most useful to individuals with little to no knowledge of the respective communities.
