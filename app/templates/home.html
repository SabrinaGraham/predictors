Overview

This application allows its users to contribute to the crime prevention and mitigation initiative within our country. Users may utilize the crime reporting form to submit reports to the relevant security authorities for investigation and tracking. This allows for a more faster, efficient and convenient crime detection process. Therefore, the submission of crime reports is paramount to the cause!

How to stay safe


The user's safety is of utmost importance and as such is our first priority in the implementation of this application. We encourage all our users and stakeholders to utilize the data and resources presented here for their safety and consideration. The app provides relevant crime information of specific divisions within Jamaica that the user can use as reference point and to remind them to stay vigilant in in crime prone areas. This is most useful to individuals with little to no knowledge of the respective communities.


Other Tips:

  1. Don’t Travel Far After Dark - If you are staying in a hotel, it is best for you to be back by nightfall. Whether you are traveling by foot or car, it isn’t advised to be out on the street at night.

  2. Travel in Numbers - Even if you are in the safest place to live in Jamaica, walking around freely isn't a great idea. While traveling alone during the day isn’t particularly unsafe, it is definitely advised against at night. It is best for tourists to always travel in numbers at all times. Traveling as part of a pack will make you less of a target and will help you to become more aware of your surroundings.

  3. Carry Cash, but Just Enough - To avoid having to use ATM’s and credit cards, it is best to carry cash. However, you don’t need to take all of your spending money with you everywhere you go.

  4. Don’t Seek Out Criminal Activity - f you go looking for criminal activity, you easily make yourself a victim of a more violent crime that you essentially can’t report because of your actions. Avoid this situation altogether by sticking to strictly legal behavior.

  5. Do not flash expensive bling - Eyes are everywhere and it could make you a mark as someone "rich".
